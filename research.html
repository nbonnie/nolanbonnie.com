At Sandia National Laboratories, I provide AI and ML solutions to complex cybersecurity emulation problems. At the moment, I am leveraging reinforcement learning to calculate optimal defensive structures for computer networks, and training convolutional neural networks to classify malware families.

Currently I hold the position of R&D S&E - Cybersecurity, as a member of the technical staff. As an employee at Sandia, I get the opportunity to work with some of the brightest scientists and engineers in the world, and I'm incredibly proud of the results my team has produced. Some of my previous projects included the development of a novel AI Planning agent, solving dynamic graph traversal problems, and deploying big data analytics on over 6 petabytes of network traffic data.
